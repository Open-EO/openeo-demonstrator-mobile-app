Timespan

As the satellites will only pass over a specific location every couple of days, the app always uses an average calculated over the last couple of days before the seletced date. By setting the range to a higher number, you can ensure that at least one pass is available for each location.

Of course, the length over which the average is calculated can be adjusted as well (default is 10 days).
